English Club


English Club

Objective

To enable students converse fluently To develop the four skills LSRW (Listening, Speaking, Reading & Writing) and use them effectively. To facilitate students acquire proper listening & Speaking Skills. The English club aims to develop all the four fundamental skills that are given due importance with the view of making students gain maximum knowledge and also develop their communication Skills. The students will be provided with different activities like puzzles, Spell bee, crossword Puzzles, Extempore, Just a Minute, conversational Practice, Seminars, role play, Group Discussion, Description. The activities would enable them to sharpen their cognitive and analytical skills and also improve their proficiency in English and understanding of the similarities and differences between English and their Mother tongue.


Vision

To provide an effective oral and written communication for the members to competently develop their English Language skills.


Mission

To provide opportunity to develop their skills and explore their abilities in Reading, Writing, Speaking and Listening. To develop English learning programs and involve students participate in competitions. To encourage students to speak English and promote English speaking environment.
