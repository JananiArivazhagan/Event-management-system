Energy Efficiency – A way to Manage and Limit rapid Energy Consumption!

Energy consumption has incredibly increased over the last few decades. We are inducing devastation to our planet’s natural resources by using it plentifully and also it pollutes the atmosphere and creates demand for pure breathing air. Energy efficiency is a way of managing and limiting this rapid energy consumption. It is to safeguard the planet and its wildlife habitats and to ensure, there is some energy left for future generations. A healthy discussion between a group of energy experts across the globe on energy consumption and natural resources came for a conclusion to form a research forum named ENergy Efficiency Research Group (GREEN9) in the year 2016 and initiated the energy efficiency activities from 18th June, 2016. In the year 2019, GREEN9 affixed it with a Registered Professional Society named as “The Institution of Green Engineers” (IGEN) (Registered under Societies Registration Act 27 of 1975, wide Registration No: CN/SR/3032019)

OBJECTIVES OF IGEN GREEN9

 To produce skilled Energy Engineers through G9EnSAv Club – CEnA (Certified Energy Ambassador)  To inline the activities towards UN Sustainable Development Goals 7 & 13  The quest to reduce the Carbon dioxide footprint by reducing energy demand through effective energy audit  To design – develop – implement innovative products which can provide solutions for energy efficiency  To promote save energy/renewable energy awareness to the general public.
